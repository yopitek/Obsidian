retreat #flashcard  
撤退；退卻；靜修地  
explanation: to move back from a place, especially because you are being attacked / a quiet place where you can go to rest  
sentence 1: The army was forced to retreat.  
sentence 2: She decided to go on a meditation retreat.

revel in #flashcard  
陶醉於；盡情享受  
explanation: to enjoy something very much  
sentence 1: She reveled in the attention.  
sentence 2: They reveled in their newfound freedom.
