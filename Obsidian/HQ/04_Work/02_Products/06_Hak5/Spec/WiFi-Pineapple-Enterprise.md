# WiFi Pineapple Enterprise

> Full spectrum enterprise WiFi pentesting. Built for the most demanding airspace with quad-core CPU, 5 dual-band radios, and AC power.

## Overview

The WiFi Pineapple Enterprise is the high-performance, rack-mountable version of the WiFi Pineapple platform. Designed for cybersecurity agencies, pentest firms, and enterprise environments requiring long-term deployments, high-throughput backhaul, and support for hundreds of simultaneous clients.

- **5 Dedicated Radios** — 2.4 + 5 GHz for high-density environments
- **Dual Gigabit Ethernet** — High-throughput WAN backhaul
- **Patented PineAP Suite** — Open, WPA-PSK, and Enterprise rogue AP attacks
- **Cloud C² Plug-and-Provision** — Ship device to client site, access remotely
- **Campaigns & Reports** — Automated active & passive WiFi pentests
- **Allow/Deny Filtering** — In-scope compliance by MAC and SSID
- **Rack-Mountable** — 1U form factor for permanent deployments
- **SSH & Web Shell** — Full Linux access
