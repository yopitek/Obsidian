# USB Rubber Ducky

> To a human it's a flash drive. To a computer, it's a keyboard typing at superhuman speeds.

## Overview

The USB Rubber Ducky is Hak5's iconic keystroke injection tool, originally introduced in 2010. Now in its second hardware generation (2022) with DuckyScript™ 3.0, it remains the world's most recognized HID attack platform. Computers trust keyboards as human input devices — the Rubber Ducky exploits this trust to deliver payloads at superhuman speed.

## Hardware Generations

| Generation | Year | Notes |

| Mark I | 2011 | Original hardware, DuckyScript 1.0 |
| Mark II | 2022 | New hardware, DuckyScript 3.0, USB-A/C dual connector |

## DuckyScript Versions

| Version | Year | Features |
|---------|------|---------|
| 1.0 | 2010/2011 | Sequential keystroke + delay |
| 2.0 | ~2017 | Device-specific, Bash Bunny era |
| 3.0 | 2022 | Full structured language: variables, if/else, while, functions, extensions |

## DuckyScript 3.0 New Features

- **Control flow** — `if/then/else` conditionals
- **Loops** — `while` repetition
- **Functions** — Reusable payload blocks
- **Extensions** — Third-party add-ons
- **HID & Storage attack modes**
- **Keystroke Reflection** — Read what was typed back
- **Jitter & Randomization** — Evade timing-based detection
- **USB-C support** with Flash Drive Case
