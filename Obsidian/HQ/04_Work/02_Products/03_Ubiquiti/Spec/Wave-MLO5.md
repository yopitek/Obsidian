# Wave MLO5
## 5 GHz Multi-Link Point-to-Point Outdoor Bridge

### Overview
Outdoor point-to-point wireless bridge with **5+ Gbps throughput** and up to **100 km range**. Dual 5 GHz radios with 2×2 MIMO each. Pole-mount design with IPX6 weatherproofing and integrated GPS. Ideal for building-to-building backhaul links.
