# UniFi U7 Long Range
## WiFi 7 Dual-Band Long-Range Indoor Access Point

### Overview
WiFi 7 **dual-band** AP (no 6 GHz) optimised for long-range coverage. Larger 160 m² footprint than U7 Pro with lower power draw (14W via standard PoE). Best for open floor plans and warehouses.
