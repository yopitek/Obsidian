# UniFi U7 Pro Max
## WiFi 7 Tri-Band High-Density Indoor Access Point

### Overview
High-density WiFi 7 tri-band AP with 4×4 MIMO on 5 GHz. Supports 500+ clients. Ideal for high-density environments such as conference rooms, classrooms, and open offices.
