# UniFi U7 Pro
## WiFi 7 Tri-Band Indoor Access Point

### Overview
Entry-level WiFi 7 tri-band indoor AP with 2.5 GbE uplink. Covers up to 140 m² and supports 300+ clients. Ideal for home and SMB deployments.
