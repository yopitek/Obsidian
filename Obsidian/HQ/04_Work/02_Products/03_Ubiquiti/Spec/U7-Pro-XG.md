# UniFi U7 Pro XG
## WiFi 7 Tri-Band Indoor AP with 10 GbE Uplink

### Overview
WiFi 7 tri-band AP featuring a **10 GbE uplink** for high-throughput environments. Thinner profile (32.5 mm) than U7 Pro. Covers 140 m² with 300+ clients.
