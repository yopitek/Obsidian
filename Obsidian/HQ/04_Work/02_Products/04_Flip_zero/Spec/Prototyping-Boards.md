# Prototyping Boards for Flipper Zero
## 3-Pack DIY GPIO Breakout PCBs

### Overview
Bare prototyping PCBs specifically designed for Flipper Zero's GPIO header. Enables makers and developers to build custom DIY modules and accessories without starting from a blank board. Includes both small and large breakout board sizes with unsoldered pin headers.
