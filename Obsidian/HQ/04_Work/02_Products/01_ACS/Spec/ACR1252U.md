# ACR1252U
## USB NFC Reader III — NFC Forum Certified

### Overview
NFC Forum-certified PC-linked reader on 13.56 MHz contactless technology. SAM slot for key diversification and mutual authentication. Supports all three NFC modes: card reader/writer, card emulation, and peer-to-peer. FIDO authentication support for YubiKey/PocketKey. Post-deployment firmware upgrade supported.

Available variants:
- **ACR1252U-M1** — USB Type-A connector
- **ACR1252U-MF** — USB Type-C connector
