# APA-M25-6E
## WiFi 6E Indoor Directional Antenna — Tri-Band 2.4/5/6 GHz

### Overview
WiFi 6E capable directional panel antenna covering all three bands including the new 6 GHz band. Companion antenna for Alfa WiFi 6E USB adapters (AWUS036AXML, AWUS036AXM). Same panel body dimensions as APA-M25 but adds 6 GHz coverage.
