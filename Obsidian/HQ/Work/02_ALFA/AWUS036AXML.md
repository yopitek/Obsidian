# AWUS036AXML
## WiFi 6E Tri-Band USB-C Adapter with Bluetooth 5.2

### Overview
Alfa's flagship WiFi 6E tri-band USB adapter operating simultaneously on 2.4 / 5 / 6 GHz for a combined ceiling of 3000 Mbps. USB-C connector with included screen clip. Best choice for WiFi 6E + Linux + USB-C laptops.

> ⚠️ macOS 11+ and Apple Silicon NOT supported. macOS limited to 10.15 Catalina on Intel only.
