# ARS-25-57A
## Dual-Band 2.4GHz+5GHz Paddle Antenna

### Overview
Dual-band omnidirectional paddle antenna. Slim cylindrical form with RP-SMA base connector — compatible with any Alfa adapter with RP-SMA female port. Strong 7 dBi gain on 5 GHz makes this a practical upgrade over included 5 dBi dipoles.
