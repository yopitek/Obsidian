## 🎓 Lessons Learned (Knowledge Base)

1. **Vision Persistence:** Reference images in multi-turn conversations (via `sessionId`) are not "sticky." You MUST provide the `--ref` flag in every tool call to maintain product fidelity.
2. **Batch Processing Risk:** High-quality models (Pro) are sensitive to request volume. Sequential execution is slower but guarantees 100% success rate for complex technical visuals.
3. **Prompt Anchoring:** Using the phrase "Strictly maintain the hardware silhouette" acts as a strong negative constraint against AI stylistic drift.

## 🚀 Final Recommended Workflow
1. **Model:** Always use `gemini-3-pro` for product-centric marketing materials.
2. **Method:** Generate one-by-one.
3. **Anchor:** Always include `--ref [original_product_photo.png]` in every command.
4. **Style:** Use a consistent `sessionId` for lighting and color consistency.
