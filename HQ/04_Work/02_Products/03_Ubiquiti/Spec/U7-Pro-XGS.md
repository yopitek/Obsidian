# UniFi U7 Pro XGS
## WiFi 7 Tri-Band Indoor AP — 10 GbE + 4×4 MIMO

### Overview
Top-of-line indoor WiFi 7 AP combining **10 GbE uplink**, **4×4 MIMO on 5 GHz**, and 500+ client capacity. Largest coverage footprint (160 m²) in the U7 Pro line. Requires PoE++.
