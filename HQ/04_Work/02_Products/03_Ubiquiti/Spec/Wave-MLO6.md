# Wave MLO6
## 5 GHz + 6 GHz Multi-Link Point-to-Point Outdoor Bridge

### Overview
Next-generation outdoor PtP bridge adding a **6 GHz radio** alongside 5 GHz for **8+ Gbps combined throughput** at up to 100 km range. Same rugged form factor as MLO5 with IPX6 weatherproofing, integrated GPS, and 10 GbE uplink.
