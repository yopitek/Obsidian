# UniFi Flex Mini 2.5G
## 5-Port 2.5 GbE Compact Desktop Switch

### Overview
Ultra-compact 5-port 2.5 GbE desktop switch with USB-C or PoE power. Non-blocking 25 Gbps switching fabric. Ideal for upgrading desktop workstations and media rooms to 2.5 GbE without a full rack switch.
