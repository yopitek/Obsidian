# ARS-NT5B7
## WiFi 7 Dipole Antenna — 2.4 GHz / 5 GHz / 6 GHz

### Overview
Tri-band omnidirectional dipole antenna rated for WiFi 7. Covers all three WiFi bands including 6 GHz. Excellent temperature range (−40°C to +85°C) for demanding environments. Highest 6 GHz gain (7 dBi) among the Alfa antenna lineup. Slim cylindrical form with RP-SMA connector.
