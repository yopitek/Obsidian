# APA-M04
## 2.4GHz 7dBi Indoor Panel Antenna

### Overview
Directional flat panel antenna for 2.4 GHz indoor use. Black matte finish with perforated panel design for weight reduction. RP-SMA connector for compatibility with Alfa USB adapters. Fixed mounting via bracket/stand.
